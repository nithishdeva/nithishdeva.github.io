ABSTRACT
Back propogation is on of the main pillars of deep learning and associated machine
learning algorithms. Here is my attempt to decipher them. The following article
is based on the approach take by Yann Le Cun in his paper [1] which follows
th eapproach of casting the problem as an optimisation problem with constraints.
Follwing which the legrangian function of the problem is formulayted and even-
tually, we derive the back propogation algorithm from it. This article can also
be viewed as an attempt to aggregate main concepts form the affore mentionaed
paper.
