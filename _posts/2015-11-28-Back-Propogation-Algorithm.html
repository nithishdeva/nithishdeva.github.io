ABSTRACT
Back propagation is one of the main pillars of deep learning and associated ma-
chine learning algorithms. Here is my attempt to decipher it but in a more theoret-
ical aspect. The following article is based on the approach take by Yann Le Cun
in his paper [1] in which back propagation algorithm is derived from Lagrangian
of the optimization problem. This article can also be viewed as an attempt to
aggregate main concepts form the afore mentioned paper.
